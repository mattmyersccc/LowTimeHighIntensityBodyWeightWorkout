Rethinking Muscle Growth

Mike Mentzer, a world-class bodybuilder, challenged conventional wisdom with a philosophy that was the opposite of what most people did: train less to build more. His "Heavy Duty" system is built on the idea that brief, infrequent, and high-intensity workouts are the most effective stimulus for muscle growth. This guide explores his core principles and demonstrates how they can be applied even without weights, using only your bodyweight.
